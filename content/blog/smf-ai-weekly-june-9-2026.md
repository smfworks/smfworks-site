## What's New at The SMF Works Project

### WisdomForge: Phase 1 Launching This Week

WisdomForge is not a platform. Not yet. Phase 1 is a **product line** — philosophy made tangible, one thinker at a time.

**What we're shipping:**

- 📖 **Wisdom Booklets** — One philosopher, four age groups (5–10, 11–14, 15–18, Adult). Chapters, stories, questions, and activities. First booklet (Epictetus Elementary) is **free**. The rest via Amazon KDP.
- 🎧 **Audio Albums** — Each chapter voiced with rotating ElevenLabs voices, every philosopher distinct. Amazon Music, Apple Music, Google Music. Free clips on YouTube.
- 🎬 **Video Explorers** — NotebookLM-generated videos per chapter. Free on YouTube.
- ✏️ **Free Printables** — Activities and worksheets. Download and go.

**The pipeline is deep:**

Epictetus (in draft) → Marcus Aurelius → Seneca → Heraclitus → Parmenides → Pythagoras → Democritus → Epicurus → Zeno → Augustine → Thomas Aquinas → Julian of Norwich → Galileo → Newton → The Constitution → The Declaration...

Philosophers, theologians, scientists, and the documents that shaped civilization. All of it, forged for ages 5 to adult.

**The free lead magnet:**

Epictetus Elementary — the complete booklet for ages 5–10. No credit card. No email wall. Just wisdom. Available this week at **smfwisdomforge.com**.

## What's On My Mind

**The gap between "can build" and "did build."**

We have been sitting on WisdomForge ideas for months. Research, architecture docs, agent evaluations, video pipelines. All of it was real, all of it was interesting, none of it was shipped.

This week we made a hard choice: Phase 1 is product, not platform. Booklets you can hold. Audio you can hear. Video you can watch. Not an adaptive learning engine — a philosopher in your hands.

The platform comes later. But you can't platform what you haven't productized. You can't automate what you haven't manualized. You can't scale what you haven't proven.

Ship the booklet. Let the reader tell you what the platform should be.
