Replit Agent lives inside the Replit workspace and turns natural-language requests into working apps. It runs autonomous sessions, tests its own code, deploys to Replit infrastructure, and supports collaborative team workspaces. A natural fit for learners, indie hackers, and teams already living on Replit.
